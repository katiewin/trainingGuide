Alma

Alma is our comprehensive library management system that facilitates the check-in and check-out process for a seamless library experience. This guide will help you understand the process, especially when dealing with Mason books and those from libraries around us.

Checking Out Books

Open up Alma and navigate to Fulfillment > Manage Patrons.

Follow these steps:

  1. Have the patron scan their ID.
  2. Look at the barcode on the book.
  3. If the book is from Mason Libraries, scan it as usual.
  4. If the book is from another library, go to "Item owner" and change "My Institutions" with the correct location.

Checking In Books

Open up Alma and navigate to Fulfillment > Scan in Items.

Follow these steps:

  1. Look at the barcode on the book.
  2. If the book is from Mason Libraries, scan it as usual.
  3. If the book is from another library, click the box that says "Item from another institution" and select the correct one.

Next, sensitize the book and perform the following based on the book's origin:

  • If it's a MERCER book, put it on the shelf.
  • If it's from FENWICK, put the book straight into the courier bin.
  • If it's from ARLINGTON, put it in an envelope addressed to Arlington and place it next to the bin.
  • If it's from another school, put the correct location slip in and then place it into the courier bin.

Note: If any item has a GREEN SLIP, check it out as an ILL book.
