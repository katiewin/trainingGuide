Closing

In this training session, you'll learn about our library's closing procedures. Every night, we initiate the closing process 30 minutes before closing time. During this period, you'll discover the essential tasks our team undertakes to ensure a smooth and efficient closing routine. Your understanding and participation in this process will contribute to maintaining the library's functionality and positive environment. Let's get started!

  • Close and lock all of the study rooms
  • Give a 15-minute warning to remaining patrons
  • Check bookdrop
  • Turn off TV
  • Lock keybox
  • Place keybox key in the top shelf
  • Return shelf key to binder
  • Lock door and return key to drawer
  • Check the library for chairs not pushed in or anything left behind
  • Turn off computers at the front desk
  • Turn off computer monitors, including the monitor used to share the screen with patrons
  • Ensure everyone has left the library
  • Clean up the front desk
